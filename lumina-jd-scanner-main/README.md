# Lumina JD | AI-Powered Skill Decoder & Career Strategist

**Lumina JD** is a high-end career intelligence platform designed to move job seekers from "average applicant" to the **top 0.1%**. Built with a professional Executive Light Theme and 3D Glassmorphism, it uses Gemini 2.0 Flash to deconstruct complex Job Descriptions into actionable data.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🔍 AI Skill Decoding
Paste any Job Description and let the AI extract, categorize, and score every requirement.
* **Skill Radar:** Visualizes expertise across Languages, Frameworks, and Infrastructure.
* **Semantic Color System:** High-priority skills are instantly highlighted (Electric Blue for Critical, Emerald for Core).
* **AI Insights:** Get a one-sentence summary of what the role *actually* demands.

### 📊 Resume Gap Analyzer
Upload your resume to see exactly how you stack up against the market requirements.
* **Match Justification:** "Why not 100%?" — Detailed point-based deductions (e.g., -10% for missing LangGraph).
* **Step-by-Step Fixes:** Get specific keywords and project ideas to bridge technical gaps.

### 🏆 Top 0.1% Winning Strategy
Don't just apply—dominate the application pool.
* **Industry-Specific Projects:** Suggests portfolio projects tailored to the company's niche.
* **Enterprise Insights:** Advice on MLOps, CI/CD, and safety guardrails for giants like Siemens.
* **Critical Requirements:** Flags Education (B.Tech/Masters), Experience levels, and shift agreements.

### 📋 Application Tracker
A dedicated dashboard to manage and monitor your career journey.
* **Score Memory:** Saves initial match scores and allows manual updates after resume editing.
* **Status Tracking:** Manage roles from "Applied" to "Interviewing" and "Offer Received."
* **Local/Cloud Storage:** Securely save your scans and progress.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** React, Tailwind CSS (Executive Light Theme)
* **AI Engine:** Gemini 2.0 Flash (via Lovable AI / Google AI Studio)
* **Backend:** Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Edge Functions)
* **Animations:** Framer Motion
* **Visuals:** 3D Glassmorphism & Semantic UI logic

---

## 📖 How to Use

1. **Paste JD:** Drop the full job text into the main decoder.
2. **Upload Resume:** Provide your resume to see your real-time match score.
3. **Consult the Playbook:** Follow the "Winning Strategy" to find out what project or keyword will bypass the ATS.
4. **Track & Improve:** Add the role to your **Application Tracker**, edit your resume, and update your score as you improve.
